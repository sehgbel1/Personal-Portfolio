Our Pride

National Anthem

All hail, hail! (All hail!)
this glorious lad of liberty
shall long be ours

Though new her name,
Green be her fame, (a)
And might be her powers,
and might be her power

In joy and gladness
With hour hearts united,
We'll shout the freedom
of a race beneghted

Long live Liberia, happy land!
A home of glorious liberty,
By God's comand!

All hail, Liberia, hail! (All hail!)
In union strong success is sure
We cannot fail!

With God above
Our rights to prove
We will o'er all prevail,
We will o'er all prevail

We heart and hand
Our country's cause defending
We'll meet the foe
With valor unpretending.
